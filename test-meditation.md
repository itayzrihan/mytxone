# Test Meditation for TTS (Short Version)

This is a short 2-minute breathing meditation designed to test the TTS functionality without hitting rate limits.

## Meditation Content:

[00:00] Welcome to this short breathing meditation. Find a comfortable position and gently close your eyes.

[01:00] Now, bring your attention to your breath. Notice the natural rhythm of breathing in and breathing out.

[02:00] Take a final deep breath in, and as you exhale, slowly open your eyes. Your meditation is complete.

---

This meditation has only 3 segments, which should work within the free tier limit of 3 TTS requests per minute.
